Cookies And Policy


Thank you for visiting Spotify. This page is to inform you about our use of cookies on this website and to explain our policy regarding your privacy.

Cookies:

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are used to enhance your user experience and to provide statistical information about how our website is used. We use cookies to personalize content and ads, to provide social media features and to analyze our traffic. We also share information about your use of our site with our social media, advertising and analytics partners who may combine it with other information that you've provided to them or that they've collected from your use of their services.

The cookies that we use on this website include:

Necessary cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functionalities such as page navigation, access to secure areas of the website, and so on. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly.

Analytical cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website. This information is used to improve the website and enhance the user experience.

Advertising cookies: These cookies are used to deliver targeted advertising to our website visitors. They may also be used by third-party advertisers to deliver advertising that is relevant to your interests.

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.
